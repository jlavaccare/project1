About The Freds

Much like the Talking Heads, the Freds were founded by four intrepid students at Rhode Island School of Design (all Class of 2007 Alums) back in 2005. Our lead singer, Freddi Arquette, had the idea to give the guys stage names ... "Fred Redd" on guitar, "Fred Orange" on bass and "Fred Greene" on the drums. They were listening to their favorite "RISD" alum band and had the idea ... hey, we can play these songs!! Then they moved to Boston together in the late 2000s, and a campus legend became a city-wide legend. They started just playing Talking Heads songs, but by popular demand expanded into the biggest hits of the 1980s and more... go check out our SETLIST for more. The gang even met David Byrne himself after a 2012 concert. We're celebrating our 20th Anniversary in 2025.

Where We Play

The Freds are based in Boston and are available to play anywhere within 100 miles of Boston Common (measured "as the crow flies") for no additional fee. If you're further than 90 miles away and interested in having the Freds play at your party - you still can! We'd say that about 3/4 of our shows are in the Boston-Providence area, but we've played in places like Maine, Syracuse, and even Ottawa and will travel! We'll charge an additional "travel fee" according to the following structure:
  • 101-270 miles from Boston Common: Additional fee of $0.75 per additional mile beyond 100
  • More than 270 miles away: Flat $500 travel fee
  • If located in Canada: Additional $75 international travel fee, then charged based on proximity to Boston Common
